Landmines are in decline globally

But their effects are still being felt

Data collected by the Landmine and Cluster Munitions Monitor and the United Nations Mine Action Team shows landmines are still a problem in many countries today. Though the world is making progress, thousands of people are injured and killed each year by landmines. Data from 1999 to 2009 gives snapshot of where landmines are most prevalent and how much is being done.
